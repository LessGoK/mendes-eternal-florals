About Us

Our Story....

At Mendes Eternal Florals, we believe that every bouquet is more than flowers — it’s a message, a memory, and a story waiting to be told. Our passion lies in crafting arrangements that capture emotion and turn ordinary days into extraordinary celebrations.

Founded with the vision of making luxury florals accessible, we blend artistry with affordability so that everyone can share beauty without compromise. Whether it’s a birthday, graduation, or a quiet “just because,” our roses are designed to last in memory and meaning.

Our Process

STEP 01

Place Your Order

You begin your experience by selecting your preferred bouquet style or requesting a custom design, and submitting your order through our simple online form so we can capture all your preferences and occasion details accurately.

STEP 02

Order Confirmation & Payment

Once your order is received, we carefully review all the details, confirm availability, and then send you secure payment instructions so your bouquet can be officially reserved and scheduled for creation.

STEP 03

Handcrafting Your Bouquet

Our team starts the process of carefully designing and crafting your bouquet with attention to detail, ensuring every arrangement is beautifully balanced, elegant, and designed to reflect the emotion behind your special occasion.

STEP 04

Delivery to Your Doorstep

After your bouquet is completed and quality-checked, it is carefully packaged and delivered to your chosen location, ensuring it arrives fresh, secure, and ready to create a memorable moment for you or your loved one.
